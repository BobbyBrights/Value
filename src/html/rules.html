Value Day 3: Decorate Our Home
Activity Rules

Phase 1: Vision

Activity Overview

Activity 1: Indoor Home Improvements

Create a concept brief proposing at least ten (10) home improvements for the floor and wing in your designated area. The red color area on the map indicates the location.

Activity 2: Outdoor Balcony Decoration

Create a concept brief proposing a design to decorate an outdoor balcony. The blue color area on the map indicate the place where your team can design a new decoration.

Note: Every design should adhere to the NEXTEV Corporate Identity (CI) guideline that you received during the Value Day 3.

l Each team (20) completes 2 activities within their designated areas.

l Each participant receives the group information including the HQ map and member list by scanning the QR code at the registration on Value Day 3.

l Other participants who did not join the Value Day 3 on November 14th have also received the QR code by e-mail.

l Team Leaders represent each team and lead the team activities.

l Each team has 2,000 RMB budget for both activities.

l Team Leaders can apply for 2,000 RMB (budget) cash advance through the workflow portal. Upon completion of the work, they should provide the related invoices.

l From Nov. 16th – 20th, we had the First Round company-wide voting . Everyone had the opportunity to rate the concepts presented in the Concept Gallery on the 1st floor of HQ with a rating of 1 star (lowest score) to 5 stars (highest score).

l All of your creations (furniture, frames, painting, etc.) should be removable and non-permanent fixtures. Phase 2: Action

l It’s Time for Action phase runs from November 30th to December 27th. Every team has the opportunity to make their concept a reality in 4 weeks.

l Each team should demonstrate how their designated area reflect the VAU working methodology and Company Values.

l Each team will receive a chat group and group page to promote their ideas through my.nextev.com portal and WeChat.

l The Second Round voting will start on December 28th . Everyone will be able to vote on the completed projects and enjoy the environments.

l Every NEXTEV colleague can rate the action / execution with a rating of 1 star (lowest score) to 5 stars (highest score) based on creativity, quality, VAU working methodology and Value integration.

l Everyone can vote for one team only, and you cannot vote for your own team. Only 6 Finalists will compete for the Final 2 Grand Prize Winners.
