An Open Talk with 'Will I am'

Hello everyone,

I’m quite sure most of you noticed the curious posters around the company about “an open talk with Will I am”

It really is your time now!No bosses! No barriers! Just William

We value truthfulness, integrity and clarity in everything we do. This allow us to be open, sincere and share our thoughts and ideas with our colleagues and most of all with the Founder and Chairman of NextEV directly!

The sessions is based on a chat and dinner with William in the VR room, which will be arranged as a familiar living room for this special occasion .

Stay tuned for a personal invitation in one of the arranged sessions. To make sure of a fruitful conversation, each session has been designed in particular for each of you.

William looks forward to meet you all!

Curiosity: Why “Will I am”?

Will I am means of course William, but is also a word play that represent different meanings, for example:

Will I am as “Hi, I am William, good to see you”

Will I am as I am William so everyone has the same William’s ownership

Will I am as Will I ma(ke) something good.

Did you find other combinations? Share them on WeChat!
